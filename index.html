About

I am a Researcher in the Optical Networking & Sensing Department at NEC Laboratories America. My work focuses on distributed acoustic sensing (DAS) and fiber-optic communication technologies, developing methods that transform optical fibers into sensitive, passive sensors for large-scale monitoring.

Research Focus

  • Distributed acoustic sensing (DAS) and distributed fiber-optic sensing (DFOS)
  • Fiber-optic acoustic recognition and AI-enhanced sensing for infrastructure monitoring
  • Advanced vibrometry and vibration sensing techniques
  • Applications: perimeter security, seismic monitoring, infrastructure protection
